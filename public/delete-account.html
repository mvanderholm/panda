Request Account Deletion

You can request that your PinPoint Rewards account and all associated data be permanently deleted. To submit a request, email us at the address below with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address tied to your account.

The following data will be deleted upon request:

  • Account credentials and profile information
  • Rewards points and transaction history
  • Saved preferences and settings
Requests are processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.
